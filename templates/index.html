About the Project

Powered by Mistral 7B, Fine-tuned for Education

Custom Fine-Tuning Process

SciQuizPro is powered by a Mistral 7B model fine-tuned on the SciQ dataset, a collection of 13,679 crowdsourced science exam questions covering physics, chemistry, biology, and other science topics. Using LORA fine-tuning with 8-bit quantization, we've optimized the model specifically for educational content generation.

The model runs locally on your machine using Ollama, ensuring privacy and control over your data. While our fine-tuned model strives for accuracy, it's important to note that AI-generated content may occasionally contain inaccuracies. We recommend that educators review all generated questions for educational accuracy before using them in the classroom.

Local Processing

SciQuizPro runs entirely on your local machine using Ollama, providing high-quality educational content without the privacy concerns of cloud-based solutions.

Educational Focus

Designed specifically for science education, SciQuizPro helps teachers create engaging, curriculum-aligned content that challenges students at their appropriate grade level.
